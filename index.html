Thesis MNG Rämibühl  ·  2024

Grade 6/6 — amongst the best theses in the Canton of Zürich

TL;DR: High school end-thesis: built a 4-DOF robotic arm and implemented inverse kinematics from first principles — awarded the highest possible grade and recognised among the top theses canton-wide.

End-of-school thesis at MNG Rämibühl. Designed and fabricated the arm, derived the IK equations analytically, and implemented real-time control — all from scratch.

Real-Time Geometric Inverse Kinematics for a 2-DOF Robotic Linkage

Algorithm 2021

TL;DR: Implemented a real-time, closed-form geometric IK solver for a 2-DOF planar robotic linkage — first foray into robotics kinematics.

Pure geometric approach — no iterative solver. Computes exact joint angles from end-effector position in closed form for a planar 2-link chain.
